Welcome to Bolinao Falls

Experience the beauty of one of Pangasinan’s hidden gems

About Bolinao Falls

Bolinao Falls 1, 2, and 3 are clusters of beautiful waterfalls located in Brgy. Samang Norte, Bolinao, Pangasinan. Bolinao Falls 1 is about 30 mins drive from the town proper. While the two other waterfalls are just minutes away from the first waterfalls.

Why visit? Relaxing natural scenery, swimming, and photography opportunities.

Best Time to Visit: Dry season (November to May) to enjoy full water flow and safer trails.

History & Facts

  • Bolinao Falls has been a popular tourist spot since the early 2000s. Bolinao Falls 1, 2, and 3 are top tourist spots in Bolinao Pangasinan. These 3 waterfalls are connected to each other and they are also connected to Balingasay River. The “falls” system is part of a stream to which is the water from Balingasay river is flowing from, then down to the West Philippine Sea.
  • Bolinao Falls 1 is the nearest waterfalls among the three. It has a small yet deep catch basin but still spacious enough for guests to swim, kayak, and bamboo rafting. Also, Bolinao falls 1 has the highest cliff among the three waterfalls, about 2-storey high which is ideal for cliff diving. You can dive with or without a life vest and plunge into the clean, turquoise waters. Just make sure you are a good swimmer, because its catch basin is also deep. Known for its multi-tiered waterfalls and clean water.

How to Get There (from Dagupan)

Distance: ~83.2 km (~2hours 9mins by car/bus)

    By bus or Van
  1. From Dagupan, take a mini bus or van going to Bolinao.
  2. When you arrive at Sampaloc, Bolinao, get off the bus or van.
  3. You will see a sign pointing to Bolinao Falls.
  4. From there, ride a tricycle going to the falls.

Activities

  • Swimming in natural pools
  • Nature photography
  • Picnics and relaxation

Entrance Fee: ₱50 per person (approx.)

Personal Review

Bolinao Falls is a refreshing getaway from city life. The natural pools are clean, the waterfalls are picturesque, and the surroundings are peaceful. Highly recommended for friends and family outings.

Visiting Bolinao Falls felt like stepping into a hidden pocket of nature that’s calm, refreshing, and beautifully untouched. The moment you arrive, you’re greeted by the sound of rushing water and the cool breeze coming from the falls. The place isn’t overly commercialized, which makes the whole experience feel more natural and relaxing.
