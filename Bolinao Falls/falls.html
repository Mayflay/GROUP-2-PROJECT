Experience the beauty of one of Pangasinan’s hidden gems

About Bolinao Falls

Bolinao Falls is a stunning waterfall located in Bolinao, Pangasinan.

Why visit? Relaxing natural scenery, swimming, and photography opportunities.

Best Time to Visit: Dry season (November to May) to enjoy full water flow and safer trails.

History & Facts

  • Bolinao Falls has been a popular tourist spot since the early 2000s.
  • The area is surrounded by lush forests and natural pools.
  • Known for its multi-tiered waterfalls and clean water.

How to Get There (from Dagupan)

Distance: ~83.2 km (~2hours 9mins by car/bus)

    By bus or Van
  1. From Dagupan, take a mini bus or van going to Bolinao.
  2. When you arrive at Sampaloc, Bolinao, get off the bus or van.
  3. You will see a sign pointing to Bolinao Falls.
  4. From there, ride a tricycle going to the falls.

Activities

  • Swimming in natural pools
  • Nature photography
  • Picnics and relaxation

Entrance Fee: ₱50 per person (approx.)

Personal Review

Bolinao Falls is a refreshing getaway from city life. The natural pools are clean, the waterfalls are picturesque, and the surroundings are peaceful. Highly recommended for friends and family outings.
